About Us

Auro International FZCo (formerly Quippo Middle East FZCo.) was formed in the year 2014 as a free zone company and registered with Jebel Ali Free Zone Authority (JAFZA) pursuant to JAFZA Regulations, Dubai, U.A.E.

The division is focused on and distribution of all kind of Equipment and machinery and serves the Construction, Mining, Oil & Gas and Medical industry.
